]]> By: Pavel Davydov http://certaindoubts.com/any-good-epistemology-papers-in-2007/#comment-7883 Sat, 19 Jan 2008 08:15:48 +0000 http://fleetwood.baylor.edu/certain_doubts/?p=781#comment-7883 I think that some of the papers that stand out are:

“The Place of Truth in Epistemology” by Ernest Sosa (in an anthology on virtue epistemology)
