It’s just the normal alphabet, but “University of…” is left of Notre Dame, but still affects where Leiter alphabatizes it. That makes sense of a lot of otherwise puzzling orders in Leiter’s post. For instance, in Philosophy of Language, we get “MIT, NYU, Rutgers, UCLA, and Southern California are tops.” None are listed as #1, etc. What’s going on is that they’re listed in alphabetical order, but “Southern California” comes at the end because it’s the University of SC, even though the “University of…” is left out.
