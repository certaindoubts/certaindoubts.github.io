Comments on: New Book on Alston http://certaindoubts.com/new-book-on-alston/ devoted to matters epistemic Wed, 10 Apr 2019 16:37:28 +0000 hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.9.10 By: Tedla Woldeyohannes http://certaindoubts.com/new-book-on-alston/#comment-1654 Tue, 19 Apr 2005 23:19:09 +0000 http://fleetwood.baylor.edu/certain_doubts/?p=303#comment-1654 Yes, Heather Battaly and Michael Lynch have done a wonderful job in bringing together superb and illuminating papers from leading philosophers as they penetratingly reflect on Bill Alston’s philosophical works that span more than half a century.

Heather Battaly’s “Introductory Essay” is a highly instructive summary of Bill’s works and a terrific way of introducing, esp., new comers to the wide ranging contributions of Bill Alston.

I think and expect this deservedly timely tribute to Bill and also Bill’s latest book, Beyond “Justification”, will play pivotal role in philosophical debates for years to come.

Personally, I’ve been, particularly, fascinated by Alvin Goldman’s contribution to this volume and Bill’s response to it since I believe that metaphilosophical debates, which are becoming hot topics in philosophy these days, will have insightful departures from Goldman’s and Bill’s exchange.

An excellent tribute to a philosopher who richly deserves it.
