Still, I admit the attraction of this approach. It might be a good idea for those of us with an interest in this topic to start compiling a list of high-quality online resources on this topic at varying levels of difficulty. I know Branden Fitelson has some material on his web page (just for your students, Branden, or are we free to lift it for our own use?) and there must be a lot of other things out there, distributed across the net.
